HypnoBucket

and other Hallucination Machines

Hyno-Bucket is inspired by an art piece I saw at Art Hack Day at PNCA.

After seeing it and talking to the artist I knew I had to make my own. So I quickly modded my controllable Room LED setup to allow for precise freqency control and bundled the lights into a corner to get the needed intensity. Thus The Hullucination Corner was born. While being fun to use it was fairly clumsy.

    Parts used
  • Arduino Uno
  • 5v USB Battery
  • 2 11 led NeoPixel strips
  • 2 10k Potentiometers
  • Repurposed Telephone wire

Next Iteration involved me fitting it into a box to make it easier for people to use. This way they can put it on there face while laying down, hold it with their hands or place it on a high table and fit their face into it.
