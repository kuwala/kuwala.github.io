Learn to Learn

This is my page on learning. Here you will find links and resources. You can learn just about anything. Use the internet to find topics and courses you are interested in. There are Lots of good free sources. Don't just consume, learn things and create things.

Four most important assets in learning

  • A Vision to improve yourself
  • A Computer/Laptop/Phone with Internet
  • A Journal you write in daily
  • And Mentor(s) to turn for help
