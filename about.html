General Information

Hi I'm Daniel a new media and interactions artist. I explore technology and new emerging tools. In what ways can they be used for fun, work, or self improvement? What makes an instrument a loved tool, and what makes it a toy? I study and create with embedded electronics, drawings, games, ideas, audible and visual medias. Nothing is off limits, everything should be questioned, pushed to its extremes, mixed with others, and allowed to evolve into a better thing. Just like self improvement our instruments beg to be used, loved, and ultimately improved by the users.

Im facinated by Computers, they are our window to the internet, the ultimate tool. And so I am developing my ideas on this into projects and workshops. I want to show and share with people neat tools, toys, and principals that will broaden their views and relationships with technology.
